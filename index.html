Rules

Good luck in the tower!!

1. Choose a door.Every room hides rewards, danger, or something stranger. You rarely know which is which.
2. Use items wisely.Magnifying Glasses, Lucky Charms, Chicken Pies, Tomes, Torches, and other relics can save a run.
3. Survive bosses.Boss rooms and arenas are brutal, but they reward Golden Skulls for upgrades.
4. Remember the tower.Endings and fragments reveal hidden memories. The tower is not only a scoreboard.

What kind of game is it?

3Doors is a minimalist door-based roguelike about risk, greed, weird magic, and the feeling that the tower is smiling at you. It starts with three doors. It does not stay that simple.
